The concentration of music related clues as well as the puzzle title hints at the theme, which is given by the 3 rows with the longest entries as EUROVISION OPEN UP SIXTY-FIFTH SONG CONTEST ROTTERDAM. The sixty-fifth Eurovision was supposed to be held in Rotterdam this year but was cancelled due to Covid-19. (It is in fact planned to remain in Rotterdam for 2021.)

The bigrams are country codes of Eurovision participants (yes, including Australia, which has been a participant since 2015). By taking the number of hearts next to each row and indexing into the song submitted by the country in 2020, we get the phrase SONG SUNG IN SPANISH.

Author's Notes

AFAIK, the conception for this puzzle was the first of any in this hunt. I wrote something similar for a couple friends on teammate in summer 2019 (using Eurovision 2019). I completely redid it based on 2020 and the given answer, and I think it turned out even better. I hope this can be a homage to everything we haven't been able to do this year (though I have seen a lot more online hunts happening!).
