Trash to Treasure - Solution

Written by James Wu

Answer: GRIND TO DUST

View StatsBack to Puzzle

We are first confronted with a series of scrolls, each with two rebus picture puzzles attached. Each of these rebuses solve to a single word, and are ordered alphabetically by the word on the left side:

From here, we can observe from the words obtained and from the title of the puzzle, “Trash to Treasure”, that many of the words can be combined with the word “to”. In fact, they can all be paired up with one word from the right leading to one word from the left (with the exception of START and FINISH):

The one backwards pair is START TO FINISH, but this gives a specific order that the pages in the first half of the puzzle can be arranged, i.e:

START -> DUSK (TO) DAWN -> PREPARE (TO) FIGHT -> .. REDUCE (TO) RUBBLE -> FINISH

Furthermore, astute fans of Magic: The Gathering may recognize that each of these words make up half of a Magic: The Gathering card, specifically all from a set of cards known as “Aftermath” cards. A quick search of most combinations of these words should lead to the above for those unfamiliar with MTG.

Once we piece together that the words come from specific MTG cards, solving any remaining rebuses should become much more straightforward. From there, solvers can look at the second half of the puzzle, which consists of 11 half pages, all ripped from the left side. Since there are also 11 pages in the first section of the puzzle, these half pages can naturally pair with the left side words on the first half to obtain the name of a new Magic the Gathering card. Indexing into the names of these cards with the provided number, and ordering by the order from above gives the final extraction:
